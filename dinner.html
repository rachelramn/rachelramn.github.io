Dinner Recipes

Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams of mascarpone cheese
  • 4 egg yolks
  • ½ cup of sugar
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • 1 cup of coffee
  • 2 tablespoons of coffee liquor
  • Lady fingers (dependent on layers wanted and size of pan used)
  • Cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Take the sugar and egg yolks and whisk it and temper over a double boiler
  2. Once its tempered and cool, in a separate pot whisk the mascarpone and the cream. Combine the two pots
  3. Combine coffee and coffee liquor
  4. Dip the lady fingers into the coffee for 5 seconds
  5. Begin layering starting with lady fingers then a layer of the mascarpone mixture altering until the pan is filled.
  6. Put in the fridge for 30 minutes
  7. Take out from fridge, top with coco powder, and enjoy!

Smash Burger

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Brioche buns
  • Lettuce
  • American cheese slices
  • Tomatoes
  • 1 whole yellow onion
  • Olive oil
  • Hamburger Seasoning
  • Bacon
Instructions:
  1. Add all the ground beef to a skillet with olive oil. Cook until meet is no longer pink.
  2. In a different pan, add one whole onion with some olive oil and cook down until its translucent.
  3. Now it is time to cook the bacon! Cook the bacon in olive oil until nice and crispy
  4. Toast the buns in the oven until golden brown
  5. Now it’s time to assemble! First add the lettuce and tomatoes, then one burger patty, then a piece of bacon and on top of that add cheese and top with the sauteed onions.
  6. Enjoy!
